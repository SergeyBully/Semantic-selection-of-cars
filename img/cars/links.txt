Lytvynenko SV WEB-service of semantic selection of a car. KNU, Kyiv, 2022
Keywords: WEB-service, WEB-application, semantics, selection, development, car.
The object of the application is the process of creating a WEB service for the semantic selection of a car.
The purpose of the study: is to create a WEB-service of semantic selection of a car and theoretically describe each step of creating a service. WEB-service must quickly perform tasks, and be convenient and intuitive to use.
In the course work, the WEB services of a semantic selection of the car were analyzed and described, the main features of the creation of the WEB-service of semantic selection of the car are noted, the used technologies and principles of software creation are described, and WEB services of a semantic selection of the car are tested.
The obtained result is useful when performing similar WEB services, or implementing a semantic selection of any product in existing WEB services.
